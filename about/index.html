About

Hi, I’m Robin — a queer developer & creative tinkerer.

I build tools that merge accessibility, storytelling, and AI. My work explores how technology can empower creative communities and amplify queer voices.

Background

I’ve worked across AI development, frontend engineering, and community technology — often bringing open-source infrastructure and human-centered design together. My interests range from generative media pipelines to local queer gaming projects.

I hold a Ph.D. in mathematics, and I channel that analytical mindset into creative experimentation — building everything from AI media hubs to accessible community platforms.

Current focus

  • Developing AI dashboards, LLM agents, and creative-tool prototypes.
  • Building and maintaining DC Gaymers’ digital infrastructure (forms, event dashboards, Google Workspace).
  • Designing inclusive frontends emphasizing keyboard navigation and screen-reader semantics.
